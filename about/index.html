ABOUT

This website

Druidcdp.net hosts creative works made for fun by Jonathan "CD" Panepinto (also known by his online personas "Druid" and "Druidwastaken"); examples include "ZERO", "Paper launcher", and "Offline Repo Loader".

Why?

I enjoy programming, graphic design, and animation very much. I got a lot of inspiration from the Huang twins (CaryKH, Fernozzle, and Jacknjellify) , Alan Becker, Neal Agarwal, Toby Fox, And my friend Doca (Aka YouOrMeDraw) just to name a few. If I named all of them this would be over 600 words long. I don't exactly have a reason for making stuff other than pure passion, I don't care much about attention but support is always nice :D

History

I started making stuff on the internet when I was about 8 years old. Everything originally stemmed from Minecraft. If that block game didn't exist, this website wouldn't exist either! I was fascinated with the adventure map "Asleep" and its sequel. I remember watching PopularMMOs play it over and over. Eventually I got the game and the first thing I did was install and play the map (Yes this was on PC). It still hasn't gotten old. That map inspired me to start making my own minecraft maps! I learnt how to use command blocks, I loaded a creative world, and I got to work. Admittedly most (if not all) of these maps were terrible. Only one ever got published, and that was at least a year or two later. Regardless, I kept experimenting with other mediums, going from art and animation in MS paint, to programming in scratch, then in VScode. Eventually I convinced my parents to get me a copy of Adobe Animate which obviously was a big step up from MSpaint. I wanted to try learning the software myself, so with practice and some help from my friend YouOrMeDraw, I learnt to animate!
