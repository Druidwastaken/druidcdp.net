History

I started making stuff on the internet when I was about 8 years old. Everything stemed originally from Minecraft. If that block game diddn't exist, this website wouldnt exist either! I was facinated with the adventure maps "Asleep" and its sequal, I remember watching PopularMMOs play it over and over. Eventually I got the game and the first thing I did was install and play the map (Yes this was on PC). It still diddnt get old. That map inspired me to start making my own minecraft maps! I learnt how to use command blocks, I loaded a creative world, and I got to work. Admittedly most (if not all) of these maps were terrible. Only one ever got published, and that was at least a year later. Regardless, I kept expirementing with other mediums, going from art and animation in MS paint, to programming in scratch, to programming in VScode. eventually I convinced my parents to get me a copy of adobe animate which obviously was a big step up from MSpaint.
